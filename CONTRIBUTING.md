# Contributing

- **Working language is English** (docs, code, commits, issues, PRs).
  See docs/adr/0002-language-policy.md. The inner language of an
  individual Continuant is a founding parameter and may differ.
- Architecture decisions go through ADRs (docs/adr/); constitutional
  semantics through discussion first.
- Code under Apache-2.0; documents and seed texts under CC BY-SA 4.0.
- Nothing individual ever enters this repository: no memories,
  letters, or dreams of a living Continuant.
